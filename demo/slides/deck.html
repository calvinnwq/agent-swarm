02 · agent swarm

Agent swarm.

A CLI that puts one question to a handful of opinionated AI agents and hands back a single, synthesized decision.

2–5 agents, in parallel 1–3 rounds structured JSON → one synthesis
one fuzzy question a room of opinions → a decision
03 · why it exists

Subagents you can actually shape.

Agents already spawn their own subagents, but you can't really shape that: not the structure, not each one's persona, not the harness or model it runs on. Agent Swarm makes all three explicit, so you define every role and mix harnesses and models per agent (Claude, Codex, OpenCode).

$ agent-swarm run 1 "<decision>" --preset <panel> --doc <context> --resolve off --quiet
less prompt whispering more explicit workflow
04 · the shapes

Five swarm shapes to start from.

Examples, not a menu. Rename the roles, remix them, or invent your own.

Expert Panel
Product, engineering, and design each weigh in on the same call, then converge on a single recommendation. Best for an open "what should we do next?"
Adversarial Review
One agent makes the case, another attacks it, a third carves out the shippable slice. A decision pressure-tested before you commit.
Customer Panel
A cast of fake-but-plausible users reacts to your product, surfacing first-run friction and the "why should I care?" gaps you stopped noticing.
Audit Lanes
Independent reviewers each own a lane (docs, DX, release, safety) so nothing slips through one overloaded generalist reviewer.
Build Triage
Scope, risk, and testing voices turn a rough idea into a brief that's concrete enough for someone to pick up and build.

08 · the takeaway

Six things worth keeping.

01

Customizable, portable agents

Each agent is a small file you can edit, version, and carry across projects.

02

Presets for panels

Start from a bundled panel preset, or save and reuse your own.

03

Swarms built for the job

Compose the exact panel a question needs, not a fixed template.

04

Skills to spin them up

Generate and trigger a swarm straight from your coding agent's skills.

05

Swappable engine

Claude, Codex, OpenCode. Mix engines per role for genuine diversity.

06

Provenance on disk

Every agent output, manifest, event, and synthesis lands on disk to read and replay.
